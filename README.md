🌸 Zero-Two: The "Darling" Soundboard
Developed by Rudy

"Won't you be my Darling?" — Zero Two (002)

Zero-Two is a minimalist, "headless" Android application inspired by the main heroine of DARLING in the FRANXX. This app allows users to interact with Zero Two’s spirit directly from their home screen with a single tap.

🎧 The Experience

When you click the app icon:
1. The app launches invisibly using a Transparent Theme.
2. It immediately selects one of 83 randomized audio clips.
3. The activity finishes instantly, returning you to your home screen while the audio plays.

🛠️ Technical Highlights

• Strelizia Core: Logic housed in StreliziaActivity.kt.
• Headless Architecture: Executes in onCreate and calls finish() immediately.
• Dynamic Resource Engine: Pulls from 83 high-quality .mp3 files.
• Easter Egg: Sends a handshake to the system log: Partner found. Initializing Strelizia...
